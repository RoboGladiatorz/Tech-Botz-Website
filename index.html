Team Summary

Tech Botz, team 14086 is a FIRST Tech Challenge team founded in 2016 by four J.D. Clement Early College High School students: Victor Soto Castro, Lukhanyo Franse, Ariel Arellano, and Josiah Laster. Team 14086 is based through the non-profit organization Compass Youth Center. Tech Botz is entering its second year. There are currently fifteen members on the team and six mentors. Presently, team 14086 meets on the North Carolina Central campus.
